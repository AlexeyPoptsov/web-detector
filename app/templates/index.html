WEB.Detector is the most powerful tool for recognizing objects in images. Just upload an image, and you'll see what's in there. Usability, fast, beautiful...

IMAGE DETECTING

Detecting and counting objects in image

WEB.Detector analyzes the loaded image according to several models. Detects and counts the number of objects by class. In addition, the performance of each model is evaluated.

WEBCAM DETECTING

Online detection of objects from broadcast webcam

WEB.Detector analyzes the live feed from the webcam. Detects and counts objects. Additionally, the stream processing speed is calculated. The detection can be performed by any of the models available in the detector.
